Project Summary

This dashboard helps track and analyze student dining behavior across campus using Transact data. The tools below offer insight into health grouping trends, meal quality, and behavioral patterns using interactive Power BI reports. The goal of this application is to allow campus administrators to gain the analysis needed based on student dining habits and visualized data metrics to analyze their campus dining information. Each tool sets to help a administrator identify the eating habits, rate, and possible incentives that can overall allow them to put in place decisions that help improve student well being and overall eating habits of campus dining.

Filter Data

Allows granular filtering of dining data across date, time, and location.

Health Groupings

Displays clusters of student health profiles based on nutrition and habits. Allowing for incentive distribution to student body profiles to improve student dining habits and well being.

Dining Overview

Overview of student dining on campus broken down into timing, count, and heatmap metrics for each dining period to visualize rate of dining habits and trends of student dining locations on campus

Nutrition Analysis

Breaks down meal composition and evaluates nutritional quality per meal by Location. Calculates health score of a locations menu and overall student eating score at filtered location. User can also view a full tabular view of all location scoring metrics for comparison and data analysis.
